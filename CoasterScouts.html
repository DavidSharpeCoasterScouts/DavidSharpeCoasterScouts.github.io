Welcome to the Official Coaster Scouts Website!

This website is meant to share with you a variety of information about Coaster Scouts including learning about the man behind the channel, browse through archives of photos and videos of amusement parks and channel videos, gain insight on how many roller coasters Coaster Scouts has been on and which of those roller coasters are his personal favorites, engage in interactive coaster polls, and find ways to contact Coaster Scouts directly for interest in collaborations or for any other reason. For those who don't know, Coaster Scouts is a roller coaster enthusiast YouTube channel that strives to create quality content centered around roller coasters and amusement parks. Whether it be an amusement park music video, YouTuber roller coaster, or making up roller coaster and amusment park memes, there's something for everyone.
